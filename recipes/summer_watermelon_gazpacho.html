Summer Watermelon Gazpacho

Description

A refreshingly cool and vibrant soup, perfect for capturing the essence of summer. This gazpacho features chilled, ripe watermelon as its base, creating a naturally sweet and hydrating foundation. The watermelon is balanced with savory and tangy ingredients like tomatoes, cucumbers, red bell peppers, and a touch of red onion. Fresh herbs, often mint or basil, add a bright and aromatic touch.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed seeded watermelon
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • ½ English (seedless) cucumber - peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons minced shallot
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, or to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro
